Our Weekly Meetings with IEEE and NER

Workshop Details

  • Sign Up Here!

Every Monday in Dodge 050 at 7pm, we have a workshop run in conjunction with the Electric Racing and IEEE clubs. Below is the list of workshops this semester along with dates and short descriptions. Absolutely no experience is required. Please sign up using the big blue buttons!

  • Jan 23 - Yagi Antenna Build

    In this workshop, you will build a fully functional directional yagi antenna using PVC piping and cut up tape measures! This serves as an excellent introduction to radio and antenna design.

  • Jan 30 – Schematic Capture

  • Feb 6 – PCB Design

  • Feb 13 & 16 – Intro to soldering

    Learn an essential electrical engineering skill, soldering! We provide all of the materials and a custom instructional circuit that you will assemble and test! Uses the much easier to solder "through hole" components.

  • Feb 27 – 3D Printing

    3D Printing has evolved a ton in the past few years! We'll go over the process start to finish and get some hands on experience making your own parts! NOTE: This workshop takes place in our lab space (Hayden 503).

  • Mar 6 – Hacking RFID

    RFID is everywhere today, payments tickets and even security. We will have expert embedded pentester and PHD student Dennis Giese lead this workshop. Learn about the protocol and the attacks!

  • Mar 13 – Advanced Soldering

    While through hole components may be good enough for hobby projects, most modern devices use SMD aka "surface mount" components. You will once again build a custom circuit, this time with SMD components!

  • Mar 20 – Embedded Development

    Embedded devices are everywhere today. In this workshop you will implement some simple code on a wireless club custom embedded board using a variety of protocols.

  • Mar 27 – Make your own RF Network

    Expanding on the embedded platform, this workshop allows you to create a custom RF link between two embedded devices for high speed data transfer not using wifi or bluetooth!
